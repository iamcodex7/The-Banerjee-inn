Our Story · আমাদের গল্প

A Home Away
from Home

Nestled in the vibrant Bengal Ambuja township of Durgapur — West Bengal's industrial powerhouse — Banerjee Inn offers warm Bengali hospitality in a modern, comfortable setting. Guests have called it a place where they "felt the comfort of home."

Whether you're here for business at NIT Durgapur, health care at The Mission Hospital, or exploring the culture of the Steel City, Banerjee Inn is your ideal base.
